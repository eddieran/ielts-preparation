## The one idea that shapes everything

Overall 7.5 = the four skills **averaging ≥7.25** (which rounds up). A half-band is cheap to win in **Listening/Reading** and expensive in **Writing**. So:

- **Listening + Reading → push to 8.0.** They are your score engine, not just "warm-ups."
- **Writing → 7.0 is enough** (e.g. `8/8/6.5/7` and `8/7.5/7/7` both = 7.5). Takes the pressure off your hardest skill.
- **Speaking → 7.0–7.5**, moves fast with daily recorded reps.
- Spend scarce daily time on **Writing/Speaking technique** (they need production + feedback); keep L/R sharp with timed on-screen sets.

## Three rules

1. **Touch Writing OR Speaking every day** — they only improve by producing + getting feedback.
2. **Every rep ends in a rewrite/re-record** of the weak part. The redo moves the band, not the first attempt.
3. **Practise L/R on screen, always timed** (you're computer-delivered).
