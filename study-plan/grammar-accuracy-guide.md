# GRA 提分手册 — Your Personal Grammar Error Profile

Built from an exhaustive audit of your three Task 2 essays (practice-2 water, practice-3 childcare, practice-4 spoiling): **83 grammar errors**, collapsed into **8 recurring patterns**, ranked by how much each costs you. Every rule below has been independently fact-checked.

**怎么用这份手册:**

## 核心结论 | The one insight that changes everything

**You do not have a grammar-*range* problem. You have a grammar-*accuracy* problem.**
(你不缺句型,你缺的是把已经在用的句型写对。)

Across all three essays you already reach for the exact structures Band 7+ demands: relative clauses ("people who need water", "a society in which we can improve"), conditionals ("if water is free, who will pay the suppliers"), passive voice ("it is frequently argued"), concession ("Admittedly… nevertheless…"), correlatives ("not only…"), "too…to", impersonal frames ("It is common for X to…"), gerund subjects ("Giving… costs money").

**Almost every error sits *inside* a structure you are correctly aiming at** — a finite verb where a "for…to" frame needs an infinitive, a "which" with no noun to attach to, the passive of a verb that can't be passive.

> **结论:不用学新语法。** 只要把已经会写的句子改到无错。这是全考试里最机械、最可练、见效最快的一项 —— 也是你最近三篇里唯一还卡在 5 分的维度。

**为什么偏偏是这几类错?** 因为中文里**没有冠词**、**动词不随人称/单复数变形**、**逗号可以连句**。这三条正好对应你的三大雷区 —— 也就是说它们不是"粗心",是母语迁移,**靠语感永远查不出来,必须用下面的清单主动扫**。

## 三大雷区详解 | The Big Three (~half your errors)

### 🥇 Pattern 1 — Articles / determiners(~30 次,三篇全中)

**中文要点:每个单数可数名词前面必须有"帽子"(a/an/the/this/my/every…),裸奔即错。a 还是 an 看后面的发音,不看拼写。**

**The rule:** Every **singular countable noun** needs a determiner — usually **a / an / the**, but also *this/that, my/your, each/every*. Use **a/an** for a first or non-specific mention, **the** for something specific, unique, already-known, or superlative. Choose **a vs an by the SOUND** that follows (*a clear*, *an infant*). Before a superlative, **the** is the normal choice — *unless* another determiner is already there (*my best*, *our biggest*); superlative adverbs may drop it (*works hardest*).

**Your actual errors → fixes:**
- "marked with **price**" → "with **a** price"
- "decided by **market**" → "by **the** market"
- "**an** complete mindset" / "**An** clear illustration" → "**a** complete…" / "**A** clear…" *(看发音!)*
- "in **future**" → "in **the** future"
- "taught by **doctor**" / "for **whole** society" / "different from **adult**" → "by **a** doctor" / "for **the** whole of society" / "from **an** adult"

**Self-check:** 终稿扫读时,在**每个单数普通名词前停一下**:"a、an 还是 the?a/an 跟下一个音对不对?" 每个 most/-est 最高级前有没有 the(或 my/our)。

**Mini-drill:** 取 10 个名词(price, market, government, infant, doctor, society, future, mindset, effect, right),每个写三种形式 — *a ___*、*the ___*、裸复数/不可数(若合法)— 并说出每种为什么行/不行。

---

### 🥈 Pattern 2 — Sentence boundaries 句界错误(9 次,三篇全中)

**中文要点:两个完整句子不能只用逗号连。therefore / however / nevertheless 不是连词,前面必须句号或分号。"Because…" 半句不能单独成句。**

**The rule:** Two independent clauses **cannot** be joined by a comma alone. Use a **period**, a **semicolon**, or a **comma + coordinating conjunction** (and / but / so). Conjunctive adverbs (**therefore, however, nevertheless, more importantly**) do **not** join clauses — put a period or semicolon *before* them. A subordinate "**Because**…" clause cannot stand alone.

**Your actual errors → fixes:**
- "…is a basic human right**, therefore** every home…" → "…right**; therefore,** every home…"
- "…childcare**, nevertheless,** we are living…" → "…childcare**. Nevertheless,** we are living…"
- "…not capable of choosing**,** we should take…" → "…choosing**, so** we should take…"
- "**Because** children don't have a complete mindset…"(残缺)→ "**This is because** children do not have…"

**Self-check(你性价比最高的一个习惯):** 在**每个逗号**处,遮住一边问:"另一边能独立成句吗?" **两边都能 → 这个逗号违法**,改句号/分号,或加 and/but/so。

**Mini-drill:** 用 *therefore, however, nevertheless, so, but* 各写一个双子句句子,标点全对(副词前分号,连词前逗号),再把其中一个子句单独抄成一句,证明它确实独立。

---

### 🥉 Pattern 3 — Subject-verb agreement 主谓一致(9 次,三篇全中)

**中文要点:动词跟"真正的主语"一致,中间隔的词全部无视。用 he/they 代入一试即知。动名词做主语按单数。**

**The rule:** The verb agrees with its **TRUE subject**, even when a relative clause intervenes: *people who **need*** (plural), *knowledge that **is*** (uncountable = singular), *arguments… **lead***, and **gerund subjects are singular** (*Giving… **costs***).

**Your actual errors → fixes:**
- "people who really **needs**" → "who really **need**"
- "children **is** the most important thing" → "children **are**…"
- "the arguments presented above **leads** me" → "…**lead** me"
- "Giving everything children desire **cost** money" → "…**costs** money"

**Self-check:** 每个动词落笔前,**找到真主语(跳过中间所有词),用 he/they 代入测试**:they → need;he/it → needs。

**Mini-drill:** 写 6 个"主语 + who/that + 动词"句(people who…, a child who…, knowledge that…, arguments that…, 动名词主语…),给每个动词标单/复,并说出是哪个名词在控制它。

## 次级雷区 | Patterns 4–8

### Pattern 4 — Broken clauses & faulty connectives(7 次,三篇全中)

**中文要点:每句至少要有一个"主心骨"(主语+限定动词的主句);which/who/that 必须能指到前面一个具体名词;"the way + 从句"不加 of;"for X to do" 框架里必须用不定式。**

**The rule:** Each sentence needs **at least one** independent main clause (subject + finite verb) — more are fine if joined properly (semicolon, or comma + and/but/so). Every **which/that/who must point to a real noun**. Use "**the way** + clause" (no "of"); "**where / in which**" for places and situations; frames like "It is common **for X to** + base verb" need an infinitive.

**Your errors → fixes:**
- "If parents only give without teaching, **which** would probably make…" → "…, **they** will probably make…" *(which 没有名词可指)*
- "The way **of** their parents react" → "The way their parents react"
- "a modern society **that** we can improve our way" → "…society **in which** we can improve…"
- "It is common for parents **are satisfying**…" → "…for parents **to satisfy**…"

### Pattern 5 — Verb form 动词形式(7 次)

**中文要点:被动 = be + 过去分词;survive 这类不及物动词没有被动;need/decide 后接 to do;泛论用一般现在时。**

**Your errors → fixes:** "needs to be **feed**" → "be **fed**" · "**need learn**" → "need **to learn**" · "**have been survived**" → "**have survived**" · "everything they **desired**"(泛论语境)→ "they **desire**".

### Pattern 6 — Word form 词性混淆(5 次)

**中文要点:修饰名词用形容词,修饰动词/形容词用 -ly 副词,介词后面接名词。**

**Your errors → fixes:** "basic **economy** principles" → "**economic**" · "**frequent** argued" → "**frequently**" · "in **health** way" → "in a **healthy** way" · "in **natural**" → "in **nature**". *(词尾只是粗略线索:-ly 通常是副词,但 -y 也可能是名词 — economy, responsibility。)*

### Pattern 7 — Countability 不可数(4 次)

**中文要点:knowledge / information / advice / research / equipment 永远不加 -s、不加 a/an;真正可数的(subsidy)则必须复数或带冠词。**

**Your errors → fixes:** "**knowledges**" → "knowledge" · "take **the responsibilities**" → "take responsibility" · "provide **subsidy**" → "provide **subsidies**".

### Pattern 8 — Prepositions 介词搭配(4 次)

**中文要点:动词/名词和它的介词当一个整体背。**

**Your errors → fixes:** "trained **between** giving birth" → "**before**" · "elaborate consequences" → "elaborate **on**" · "consequences **about** that" → "consequences **of**" · "**under** affordable lifestyle" → "**within** an affordable lifestyle".
